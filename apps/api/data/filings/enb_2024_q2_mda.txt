ENBRIDGE INC.
Management's Discussion and Analysis
For the three and six months ended June 30, 2024

FINANCIAL RESULTS

Adjusted EBITDA was $4,280 million for the six months ended June 30, 2024, representing an increase of 8% from the prior year.

Funds From Operations totaled $3,650 million for the period, compared to $3,420 million in 2023.

Net Debt stood at $52,400 million as at June 30, 2024, up from $51,100 million at year-end 2023.

Interest Expense was $890 million for the six-month period.

CAPITAL PROGRAM

Maintenance capital expenditures were $380 million in the quarter.

Growth capital expenditures reached $1,850 million for the six months, focused on pipeline expansion projects.

DIVIDEND AND SHARES

Common shares outstanding were approximately 2,085 million at June 30, 2024.

The quarterly dividend was $0.91 per share, for an annual rate of $3.64 per share.

OPERATIONS

Liquids pipeline throughput averaged 3.1 million barrels per day.

Gas transmission volumes were 16.5 billion cubic feet per day.

The utility segment contributed $180 million in EBITDA for the quarter.

BALANCE SHEET

Total assets amounted to $153,200 million.

Long-term debt was $58,700 million.

Cash position was $1,200 million at quarter end.
