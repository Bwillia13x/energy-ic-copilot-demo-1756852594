KINDER MORGAN, INC.
Management's Discussion and Analysis
For the three and six months ended June 30, 2024

FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS

Adjusted EBITDA increased to $2,850 million for the six months ended June 30, 2024,
compared to $2,620 million in the prior year period.

Funds From Operations were $2,180 million for the six months ended June 30, 2024,
up from $2,050 million in the prior year.

Net Debt at June 30, 2024 was $29,400 million, compared to $28,100 million at December 31, 2023.

Interest Expense for the six months was $420 million, compared to $410 million in the prior year.

CAPITAL EXPENDITURES

Maintenance capital expenditures were $180 million for the quarter, while growth capital expenditures totaled $350 million.

The company invested approximately $950 million in growth projects during the six-month period.

SHARE INFORMATION

Weighted average common shares outstanding were 2,240 million for the quarter.

The quarterly dividend was set at $0.2875 per common share, representing an annual dividend of $1.15 per share.

Share price at quarter end was $19.85.

Net income for the quarter was $850 million.

OPERATIONAL HIGHLIGHTS

Natural gas pipeline throughput averaged 38.5 billion cubic feet per day.

Products pipeline volumes were approximately 2.1 million barrels per day.

Terminal volumes reached 8.2 million barrels.

FINANCIAL POSITION

Total assets were $68,900 million as at June 30, 2024.

Total liabilities were $46,500 million, with long-term debt comprising $30,200 million of this amount.

Cash and cash equivalents were $250 million at quarter end.

Shareholder equity was $22,400 million.
